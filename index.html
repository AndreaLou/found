Found Collective's involvement in our wedding day was the best decision we made!

Maria & Joaquim

We had a three hundred people wedding on our property with absolutely nothing on it except a patch of dirt - and they turned it into a wedding that surpassed my wildest dreams.


Kelly from day dot has been so kind and gave me a sense of peace of mind which is why I booked. The team attended site visits, supplier meetings, sent countless emails and texts to organise and set-up every single visual detail on the day.


It was the attention to detail they had that really blew me away. From ensuring that the colour of the candles, flowers, linens and the ribbon on the bonbonierre all matched, to ironing out every single crease on the tablecloths and napkins, that every single glass arch in our marquee was cleaned, ensuring that no scuffs or marks were to be made on our white floor before we got there, to the location placement of restrooms, the colour of the pots from the hired plants, organising timetables for delivery and pack down for the suppliers, to organising the run sheet on the day and the countless other details that I could never think of or ever have the time or brain space to deal with.
